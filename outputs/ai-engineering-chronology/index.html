Each milestone is a broad shift, placed where it became visible enough to change how AI software work felt.

The Arc

  • AI engineering moved from prompt craft to operating-system design: context, tools, memory, skills, sandbox, review, and telemetry.
  • The human role moved up a level: define the problem, choose the harness, judge the artifact, and improve the loop.
  • The big products now compete on surfaces and controls, not only model quality.

Evidence Boundary

  • OpenClaw is treated as an emerging open/local agent-OS signal, not as the whole market.
  • Model examples are grouped as frontier coding-model jumps; exact model names vary across sources and vendors.
  • Community and sweep sources are used for salience, while official/vendor/local raw captures anchor the claims.
