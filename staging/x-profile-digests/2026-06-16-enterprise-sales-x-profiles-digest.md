### Fivos Aresti: ABM Signal Stack And Founder-Led Marketing

Fivos's highest-signal posts map ABM signals by ownership tier: first-party data such as CRM, product usage, forms, website visitors, and replies; second-party data such as partner networks, warm intros, review sites, champion tracking, and LinkedIn engagement; third-party data such as technographics, job openings, firmographics, and funding. He also ties founder-led content to outbound: founder/team posts create engagement signals, which route into follow-up sequences, while outbound conversations feed the next content topics.

Promote into:

- `high-signal-enterprise-sales.md` for finite-TAM ABM and signal stacking.

- `agentic-gtm-campaign-workflows.md` for founder-led content plus outbound feedback loops.
