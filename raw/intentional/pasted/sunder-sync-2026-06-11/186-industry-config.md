Residential Only

Other

- SaaS, marketing, logistics, unrelated

### What Sunder Does (Value Prop)

```
Automates lease administration and tenant billing for commercial property managers:
- Extracts lease terms from PDFs automatically (rent, escalations, caps, base years)
- Calculates tenant billing (gross rent, service charges, outgoings)
- Reconciles CAM estimates vs actuals for year-end true-ups
- Flags missed escalations and approaching expiries
- Generates audit-ready billing summaries
```
