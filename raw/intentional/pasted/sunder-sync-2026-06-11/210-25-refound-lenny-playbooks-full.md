## Playbook Design Philosophy

**Evidence from structure:**

### 1. Role-First Organization
- 9 playbooks map to specific roles
- Not just topic dumps
- Acknowledges different needs by stage

### 2. Progression Logic
**Example patterns:**
- "Zero to PMF" (Startup Founder)
- "IC to people leader" (First-Time Manager)
- "Founder sales to sales machine" (Sales Leader)

Each implies journey/progression, not static state.

### 3. Stage Awareness
- Pre-seed vs. Series A (different challenges)
- First 6-12 months (time-bounded)
- Post-PMF (milestone-based)

- Founder, PM, Eng Manager: 15 skills (complex, broad roles)
- First-Time Manager, Growth, Sales: 12 skills (focused domains)
- Consistent within categories
