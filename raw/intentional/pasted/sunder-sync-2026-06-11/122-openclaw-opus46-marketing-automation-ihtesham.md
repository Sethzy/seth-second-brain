## Summary

Agency deployed Opus 4.6 across marketing workflows. Tasks that took 8-12 hours finishing in 45 minutes. 195 hours/month projected savings. Took on 2 additional retainer clients without hiring (+$14K MRR).

## 5 Workflows

### 1. Competitive Intelligence
- Feed 5-7 competitors + specific question
- Opus hunts across pricing pages, case studies, LinkedIn posts, investor presentations
- Output: 6-page competitive intel brief in 38 minutes
- **Time saved: 5 hours per analysis**

### 2. Campaign Briefs
- Feed: brand guidelines, campaign performance data, audience research, objective
- Adaptive thinking allocates more reasoning for complex strategic work
- Output: audience segmentation, 8-week content calendar, creative direction, success metrics
- **Time saved: 7-10 hours per brief**

### 3. Client Reporting
- Export CSVs from all platforms + previous month's report + client goals + template deck
- Opus processes entire data set (1M token context), identifies cross-channel patterns
- Generates explanations grounded in actual campaign changes with causation analysis
- Creates slides with charts formatted to brand guidelines
- **Time saved: 3-4 hours per client report**

### 4. Content Production at Scale
- 30-page brand voice codex eliminates generic AI writing
- First drafts requiring 40-60% less editing than previous AI tools
- Zero instances of generic marketing jargon
- **Time saved: 3-4 hours per blog, 1.5-2 hours per LinkedIn article**

### 5. Ad Copy Testing
- Fed 6 months of ad performance data
- Opus identified winning patterns (specific numbers, second-person questions, action verbs)
- Generated 15 variations: averaged 3.8% CTR vs human 2.4% CTR
- Best Opus variation hit 5.1% CTR
- **Time saved: 2-3 hours per campaign**

## Results (First 96 hours)

- 2 competitive intelligence briefs: 10 hours saved
- 1 campaign brief: 8 hours saved
- 1 client report: 4 hours saved
- 8 content pieces: ~18 hours saved
- 1 ad campaign setup: 6 hours saved
- **Total: 46 hours saved in 4 days**

## Projected Monthly Savings

- Competitive intelligence: 20 hours
- Campaign briefs: 24 hours
- Client reporting: 32 hours
- Content production: ~89 hours
- Ad copy optimization: 30 hours
- **Total: 195 hours/month (nearly 5 full work weeks)**

## Cost

- Opus 4.6: $5/M input tokens, $25/M output tokens
- January usage: ~18M input, 4.2M output tokens
- **Total cost: $195 (~$1 per hour saved)**

## Infrastructure Required

- Standardized folder structures per workflow type
- Brand voice codex (30 pages, continuously refined)
- Template libraries for common outputs
- Quality review checklists
- Weekly AI output review sessions
- Clear ownership: who directs AI, who reviews, who approves

## Learning Curve

- Week 1: Experimentation, lots of mediocre outputs
- Week 2-3: Prompt refinement, quality improvements
- Week 4+: Consistent, production-ready outputs
