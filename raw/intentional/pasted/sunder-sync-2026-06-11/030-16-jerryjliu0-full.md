## Main Thesis

The legacy "Intelligent Document Processing" (IDP) industry was about **extracting fields**.

The new **Agentic Document Processing** is about ***getting work done***.

## The Shift

### Legacy IDP

**Focus:** Field extraction
- Pull data from documents
- Structured output
- Static workflows

### Agentic Document Processing

**Focus:** End-to-end workflow automation
- Understanding documents is a **small step** in larger human workflow
- Documents arrive in packets
- Require human AND machine validation
- Need semantic reasoning and data validation
- Humans gather context and do data entry in downstream systems
- Or cross-reference context to generate reports

## Market Size

- **IDP itself:** Many billions of $$ in market size
- **Automating work:** Exponentially bigger

The shift from field extraction to workflow automation represents a **massive market expansion**.
