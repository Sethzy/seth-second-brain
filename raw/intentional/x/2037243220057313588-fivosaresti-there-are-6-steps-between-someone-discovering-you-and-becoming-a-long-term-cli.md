There are 6-steps between someone discovering you and becoming a long-term client.

Most companies only build the first 2.

STAGE 1 is traffic generation through outbound, paid ads, partnerships, and organic content.
STAGE 2 is lead capturing through lead magnets, social followers, social engagement, and landing page forms.
STAGE 3 is nurturing.

(most companies have the biggest gap right here)

We run 10 methods of nurture simultaneously:

SDR touchpoints
Retargeting ads
Newsletter
Content
Gifts
Email flows
Community
Dinner invites
In-person events.

STAGE 4 is conversion with video demos, testimonials, case studies, and free tools on the website.
STAGE 5 is qualification and closing.
STAGE 6 is retention and expansion.

We run all 6 stages before we even consider the deal closed.

This is the difference between a funnel and a flywheel.
