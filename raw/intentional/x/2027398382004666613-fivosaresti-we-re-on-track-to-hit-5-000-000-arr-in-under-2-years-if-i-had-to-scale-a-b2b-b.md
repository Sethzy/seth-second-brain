We’re on track to hit $5,000,000 ARR in under 2 years.

If I had to scale a B2B business from scratch again...

I would religiously use these 4 outbound motions:

(1) Cold calling

For Tier 1 accounts, nothing beats a phone call. Some level of trust requires conversation that automation can't replace.

(2) Email campaigns

We send 1,200 emails per day using Instantly with AI personalization that sounds human.

(3) LinkedIn campaigns

We auto-connect with qualified prospects using HeyReach.

Then retarget people who engaged with our posts.

(4) Manual prospecting

This is for our Dream 150 accounts.

We make sure we research each account for personalized outreach.

Everyone wants to automate everything.

But the best outbound combines all four pillars:
• Automation
• Manual outreach
• Cold email and DM
• Signal-based engagement

Simple, not easy.
