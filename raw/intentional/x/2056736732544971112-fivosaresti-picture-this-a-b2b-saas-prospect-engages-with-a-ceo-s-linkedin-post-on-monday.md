Picture this:

A B2B SaaS prospect engages with a CEO's LinkedIn post on Monday.

By Friday...

That prospect is in an outbound sequence with a personalized message tied to the engagement.

The play looks like this:

1. Jungler watches CEOs' posts and flags engagements from accounts that match the ICP.
2. The signal routes into HeyReach with the engagement context attached.
3. HeyReach sends a personalized connection request and runs a DM sequence.
4. Replies route into Slack with the original engagement context, ready for a human to take it from there.

The whole loop runs Monday to Friday.

The lift comes from treating LinkedIn engagement as a CRM event.

Every signal routes back to outbound the same week.
