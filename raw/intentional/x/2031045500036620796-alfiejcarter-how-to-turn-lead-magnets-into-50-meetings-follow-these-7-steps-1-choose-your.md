How to turn lead magnets into 50+ meetings.

Follow these 7 steps. ↓

1/ Choose Your Lead Magnet Type

→ Educational Playbook: Step-by-step system (use when selling implementation services)
→ Template Library: Copy-paste resources (use when selling creative services)
→ Audit Framework: Diagnostic tool that reveals gaps (use when selling consulting)

2/ Pick the Right Topic Framework

→ System/Framework (example: "The 72-Hour Lead Response System")
→ Tool Stack (example: "The $200/Month Outbound Tech Stack")
→ Benchmark/Data (example: "2025 Cold Email Reply Rate Benchmarks")
→ Template Library (example: "67 Cold Email Subject Lines with open rates")
→ Diagnostic/Audit (example: "The 47-Point Email Deliverability Audit")

3/ Choose Format

→ Notion: best for playbooks and systems
→ Google Doc: Universal access, easy to copy (best for templates and checklists)
→ Landing Page: Full analytics control, professional branding (best for gated content)

4/ Structure Content in 3 Parts

→ Part 1: Quick Win (under 10 minutes to implement, builds trust)
→ Part 2: The System (step-by-step with screenshots, exact tools, formulas)
→ Part 3: How to Scale It (show what complexity looks like at scale, makes your service obvious)

5/ Set Up Hosting and Tracking

→ Create page in Notion or Google Docs
→ Set sharing to "Anyone with link can view"
→ Use @Bitly or @Dub for branded short link
→ Track clicks (Dub and https://t.co/pPyPmRV8nP track automatically)

6/ Automate Delivery with DM Scripts

→ Set up a Prosp campaign
→ Extract the commenters of your post using Prosp
→ Send DM:

For connected prospects:

"Hey [first name], here's the [resource name]: [link]"

For not connected:

→ Reply to comment: “sent you a connection request to shoot it over”
→ Send them a connection request
→ Once they accept, DM the resource

7/ Follow Up

→ For ICP, follow up with a specific question

This is just one way you can turn lead magnets into meetings.
