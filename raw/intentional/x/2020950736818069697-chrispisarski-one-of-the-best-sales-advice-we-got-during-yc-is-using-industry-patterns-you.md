## Verbatim Text

one of the best sales advice we got during yc is using "industry patterns"

you should let your prospect know early that you work with teams in their specific industry & understand their problems

"we work with a lot of teams in [industry], and usually they struggle with [problem a] or [problem b]. does that match what you're seeing, or is it something different?"

prospects should understand that going with you does not just solve their current problem, but also saves them time by learning from similar companies

we asked our customer with the most referrals why he is so proactive in recommending us, and the main reason was that we helped him optimize his workflow based on what we see across the industry, instead of just solving his initial problem
