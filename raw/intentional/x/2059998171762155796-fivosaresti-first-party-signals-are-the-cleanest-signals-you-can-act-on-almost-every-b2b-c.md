## Verbatim Text

First-party signals are the cleanest signals you can act on. 

Almost every B2B company is sitting on 8 of them...

But only acting on MAYBE 2 of them.

These 8 fire from systems you already own:

1) Website visits to pricing, compare, and demo pages
→ Route through Warmly or RB2B to deanonymize the visitor at the contact level.

2) Form submissions across the site
→ Capture every fill in HubSpot or Salesforce so the rep sees it the morning after.

3) Email opens, clicks, and replies on past outbound
→ Recycle warm openers from Instantly into a re-engagement track instead of letting them die.

4) Product usage events from free trial or freemium users
→ Pipe trial activity into HubSpot as timeline events so AEs can tier accounts by depth of use.

5) CRM activity and lifecycle stage changes
→ Anyone moving from MQL to SQL needs a manual touch from the owning AE.

6) Customer alumni movements out of closed-won accounts
→ Run Clay against closed-won accounts to find former employees who just moved.

7) Mentions inside sales call recordings of competitors, problems, or champions
→ Use Ergo to surface competitor mentions across last week's calls and push them to Slack.

8) Newsletter and content download signups
→ Score each download by ICP fit, then auto-route Tier 1 signups into a HeyReach LinkedIn sequence

Start here before you pay for third-party data.
