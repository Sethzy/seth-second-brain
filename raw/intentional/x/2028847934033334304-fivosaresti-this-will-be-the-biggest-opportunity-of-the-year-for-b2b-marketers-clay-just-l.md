## Verbatim Text

This will be the biggest opportunity of the year for B2B marketers: 

Clay just launched ads.

The problem with B2B ad targeting on LinkedIn and Meta:

→ They’ve always been limited by platform-native audience matching
→ They rely on basic list uploads (CSVs of emails) that typically get 30-45% match rate
→ Which means you're wasting half of the potential of your campaigns. 

With Clay ads, you can now: 

→ Take a contact
→ Run it through multiple providers 
→ Find hashed advertising IDs and get match rates of 90%+ on LinkedIn and 60%+ on Meta.

On top of that sync it automatically to your ad platform. 

Some of the best use cases I see with this:

1. ABM Ads 

Build lists of entire buying committees at Tier 1 accounts and target the same accounts sales is prioritizing

2. Website retargeting

Deanonymize visitors with RB2B (accounts + contacts) and run re-targeting campaigns

3. Nurture existing deals

Import active opportunities from your CRM and run BOF ads to reduce sales cycle length

TL;DR:

Your ad campaigns are only as good as the people who see them.

I’m super bullish on this.
