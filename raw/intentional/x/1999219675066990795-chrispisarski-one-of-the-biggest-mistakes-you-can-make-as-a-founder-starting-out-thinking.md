## Verbatim Text

One of the biggest mistakes you can make as a founder starting out:

thinking you need to be on all platforms: TikTok, LinkedIn, X, podcasts, Reels, YouTube,..

All the successful founders posting everywhere have big content teams behind them taking care of everything.

You can't afford to waste your time doing the same at the start.

Just pick 1 or 2 platforms and try to really understand how to gain momentum there.

The best way to pick one is by just asking your customers where they spend their time.

And be as specific as possible.

At Crustdata, we offer a real-time data API (headcount, hires, web traffic, reviews,...) to developers building sales & recruiting tools.

So where do you think our customers spend their time?

We just asked them.

And you should do the same.
