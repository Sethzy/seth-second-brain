## Verbatim Text

My company 2x’d its MRR in January. 

5 things I’d attribute this to:

(that every business should understand)

1. Timing

January is the best month for B2B because companies have:
• New goals
• New budgets
• Fresh energy

We knew this was coming and prepared our pipeline for it.

2. Content momentum

Our LinkedIn content had been stacking up for months, which helped us to stay top of mind when decision-makers had a budget to spend.

Consistent posting compounds, even if you don’t see the results immediately.

3. Partnerships

We published an article with one one the most respected persons in GTM that built massive trust.

People read it and came straight to us.

Just another example of building authority through thought leadership paying off.

4. Referrals (the most underrated channel)

We built a referral program and started getting some of our best clients through it.

But the real referrals came from our actual clients.

When you do great work, clients introduce you to other people who need your services.

5. Deals reopening

Some prospects who ghosted us months ago came back.

I guess it’s the “New year, new goals” effect.

Takeaway:

Success compounds when you keep showing up.
