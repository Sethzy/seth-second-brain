Bad TAM mapping:

• B2B SaaS
• 50-200 employees
• $5M-$20M revenue

Good TAM mapping:

• Start at your CRM - analyze closed/won and closed/lost
• Look for qualifying and disqualifying signals
• Map your broad TAM
• Bring into Clay for account enrichments
• Score based on headcount, industry, location, technographics, company structure
• Back-test against closed/won to refine over time
• Segment into 3 tiers (Tier 1, 2, 3)

TL;DR: 

Your TAM needs to be more than a Sales Navigator filter.
