We received 780 inbound demo requests in the last 60 days as a seed stage startup. Here are 4 things we did to make this happen:

1. Discipline - We have certain metrics to hit on channels and we make sure we hit them no matter what. This discipline ensures we have great weeks and good weeks of pipeline, but never bad weeks. The two weeks we let these slide, we saw the hit.

2. Long Game - We bet on inbound from the very beginning. We are active on channels our competitors haven’t tapped into, ensuring visibility everywhere our prospects might be spending their time.

3. Personal Brand -  I started taking founder-led marketing seriously by writing content on LinkedIn consistently this year and I’ve gone from 1.5k to 13k followers in 8 months.

4. Power law posts - You can and should post consistently, but the reality is that a few posts will bring in 10x more attention and demos than standard posts. We went viral for our take on Lovable using Crustdata’s web traffic data and had good coverage due to our seed announcement.

Opportunity: we’re hiring for 2 more account executives in our NYC office. There will be plenty of inbound leads to work off of, but also an opportunity to build out our outbound playbook with some already existing successful use cases.
