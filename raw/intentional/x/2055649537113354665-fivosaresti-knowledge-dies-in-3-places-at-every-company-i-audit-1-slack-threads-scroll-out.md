## Verbatim Text

Knowledge dies in 3 places at every company I audit:

1. Slack threads scroll out of reach after a week.
2. GDoc pages haven't been updated in 6 months.
3. People's heads walk out the door whenever someone leaves.

They have decent docs, SOPs, Looms for every tool, onboarding checklists...

Yet, new hires STILL take 6 weeks to ramp.

The problem is where they all live.

Every system you're using is good at storing info for a person to read later.

It's bad at making that info available the moment Claude or a teammate needs it.

Move the knowledge somewhere Claude can pull from on every session, or your AI stack can't deliver.
