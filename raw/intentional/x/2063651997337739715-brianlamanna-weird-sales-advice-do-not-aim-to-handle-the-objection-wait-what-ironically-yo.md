## Verbatim Text

Weird sales advice - Do not aim to handle the objection.

Wait, what?

Ironically, you will get much better at overcoming objections, if you don't just try to handle it.

I blame the name "objection handling" for why so many reps struggle.

This isn't cold calling tae-kwon-do.

Put yourself in the prospect's shoes.

They just answered a call from a number they didn't recognize while in the middle of something.

They might think it's their children's school. 

Or a pharmacy. 

Or work.

While confused about who is calling, you come in with a perfect pitch about their company, your research, and why they should agree to a meeting.

Oftentimes, they just hope to get off the phone and think of the first objection that comes to mind instead of just hanging up (fear of being rude).

That first objection is often not very real or at least not the root cause.

If you handle a not real objection, it won't get you anywhere other than hearing a 2nd, not real objection.

Give this a go next time you get hit with one!
