## Verbatim Text

if you are doing a demo trying to sell your product with multiple people in the call, you have to make sure to:

1) manage expectations: "we have 5 people here, all with unique challenges and needs. The biggest hurdle is addressing everything at once, so we’ll focus on the core goal and do individual 1-1s later. does that sound fair?"

2) assuming you had a 1-1 call before, present a 1-slide summary where you recap the specific pains and goals your prospect mentioned so far

3) make it very interactive: after the slide, ask a specific person: "jeff, what’s your take on this? Does this reflect what you’re seeing?"

a good sign you are doing well is when prospects start discussing the product among themselves during the call

picked up this great advice from chris orlob back in the day, still holds up
