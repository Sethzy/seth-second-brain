The 8 first-party intent signals every B2B team owns:

- Website visits to pricing, compare, and demo pages
- Form submissions across the site
- Email opens, clicks, and replies on past outbound
- Product usage events for free trial or freemium users
- CRM activity and lifecycle stage changes
- Customer alumni movements out of closed-won accounts
- Mentions of competitors or champions in sales call recordings
- Newsletter and content download signups

These are the cleanest, highest-intent signals because the prospect is already interacting with your business.

Do NOT let them sit unactivated inside the CRM and the product backend.
