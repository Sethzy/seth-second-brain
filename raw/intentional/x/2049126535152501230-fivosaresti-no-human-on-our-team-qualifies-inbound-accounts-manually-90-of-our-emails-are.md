NO human on our team qualifies inbound accounts manually.

90% of our emails are validated before a rep touches a single record.

Here's the full sequence:

1. Account qualification
- Website visitors and LinkedIn engagement flow into Clay.
- Clay auto-qualifies against our ICP model.

2. Contact expansion
- Apollo finds additional decision makers in the same account.
- We map the full buying committee.

3. Email validation
- Findymail finds and validates 90% of emails.
- BetterContact enriches for phone numbers.

4. Routing
- Tier 1 accounts get a Slack alert for manual outreach.
- Everyone else goes straight into automated sequences.

By the time a rep sees the account, it's already qualified and enriched.

The manual research step disappeared.
