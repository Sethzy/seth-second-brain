## Verbatim Text

New sellers misunderstand credibility & confidence in sales.

They think it means having all the answers.

It doesn't.

It’s knowing you'll get all the CORRECT answers.

And doing what you said you’ll do.

𝗛𝗲𝗿𝗲'𝘀 𝘄𝗵𝗮𝘁 𝘁𝗵𝗮𝘁 𝗮𝗰𝘁𝘂𝗮𝗹𝗹𝘆 𝗹𝗼𝗼𝗸𝘀 𝗹𝗶𝗸𝗲:

→ You get an integration question you've never heard. You don't freeze or fake it.

You say "great question, I’m actually not sure. Let me get back to you by EOD tomorrow" - and then you do exactly that.

→ You come out of a call with 6 open action items. You close every single one before the next meeting.

𝗔𝗻𝗱 𝗶𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗿𝗻𝗮𝗹𝗹𝘆...

→ You make the people helping you feel valued. You prep them before the call. You brief them on the deal. You thank them after. You make them want to jump back in the next time you ask.

Reps build it. Diligence reinforces it. Follow-through earns it.

Show up, do what you said you'd do, and pull the right people in at the right time.

That's the whole formula.
