## Verbatim Text

I’ve been running my company for 10 months. 

ONE thing I wish I'd shipped sooner:

We turned 70% of our SOPs into Claude skills.

→ A text SOP describes the work.
→ A Claude skill executes the work.

The new way:

1. The SOP becomes a markdown skill in our GitHub repo.
2. Claude reads the skill the moment it's triggered.
3. Claude executes the steps and reports back.
4. When someone fixes Claude's output, the correction becomes a note inside the skill.

The other 30% of our SOPs still need human judgement that's hard to script.

The daily work that used to live in someone's head now lives in a folder Claude can pull from on every session.

Documentation used to be a record for a person to read later.

It's now the interface AI uses to do the work.
