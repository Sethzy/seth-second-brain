If your form only captures complete submissions, you're leaving 5-6 figures in pipeline per month on the table.

Partial Tally submissions used to disappear for us.

Now they route to Clay and then Slack automatically.

That lead is gone forever unless you have a capture system.

Here's how we route partial submissions:

1. Tally + Cal(.)com routing
- Full submissions book directly on Cal(.)com.
- Partial submissions get routed to Clay.

2. Clay enrichment
- Clay enriches company data and updates HubSpot.
- A Slack notification fires for every partial submission.

3. Follow-up
- Tier 1 accounts get manual outreach within 24 hours.
- Everyone else goes into InstantlyAI sequences.

With 7,000 monthly website visitors, even a small percentage of partials adds up.
