## Verbatim Text

AI business pricing cheat sheet 🤖

Different AI businesses need different pricing logic but most founders get this wrong.

AI startups won’t fail because of product.

They’ll fail because of pricing.

Design it intentionally.
----------------------------------------
1️⃣ AI cost ≠ SaaS cost

Your marginal cost doesn’t go to zero.
Unlimited plans can quietly destroy margins.

→ Gate usage aggressively.

2️⃣ Price based on the buyer

Developers prefer usage-based.
Teams prefer per seat.
Revenue tools prefer outcome-based.

→ Price for who signs the check.

3️⃣ Unpredictable bills kill trust

Usage-based pricing creates anxiety without guardrails.
Confusion lowers conversion.

→ Add caps, visible credit tracking, and bundles.

4️⃣ Hybrid models win

Base subscription creates stability.
Usage protects your margins.

→ Combine predictability with scalability.

5️⃣ Mission-critical ≠ token pricing

Legal, medical, and finance tools reduce risk, not tokens. High-trust products shouldn’t feel metered.

→ Anchor pricing on ROI and risk reduction.
----------------------------------------

Save this before you ship. 🔖
