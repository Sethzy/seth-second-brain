## Verbatim Text

one of the things we learned during YC is that there is nothing worse in sales than having a calendar full of “zombie deals”

you need to find a way to disqualify prospects much faster, while still having clear next steps if you think it could be relevant for them in the future:

“it sounds like you don’t actually have a massive pain point right now. am I missing something, or does it make more sense to chat again in six months when this is a higher priority?”

prospects will either

1) push back if they are a real fit
2) thank you for saving their time. if so, still keep them in the loop. many unqualified prospects later turned into customers once they hit a certain point of scale
