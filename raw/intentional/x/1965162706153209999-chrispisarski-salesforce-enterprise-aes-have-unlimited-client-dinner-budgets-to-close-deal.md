## Verbatim Text

Salesforce Enterprise AEs have unlimited client dinner budgets to close deals. 

Our new team member (ex-salesforce AE) would basically email “Lunch on me?” to his prospects. This was his ‘foot in the door’ moment.

Then it’s game on.

Your goal in the free lunch:
1) figure out who is important in the buying process
2) get a way in to these people for a second free lunch or dinner

After that, it’s just a cascading waterfall of meet ups at their favorite places. People don’t say no to this. 

You keep meeting with more and more people until the deal closes. 

Startups with a high enough ACV should think about adopting this more, especially when trying to move into mid-market/enterprise.
