i just talked with a founder who hired and scaled a massive sales team, and he told me that his biggest problem was retaining great SDR talent (people who are great at cold calling, sending emails, etc.) that are not ready for an AE role yet to run discovery calls and close deals

he said he ended up implementing a promotion system for them, he calls it “SDR Level 1”, “SDR Level 2”, etc., based on how long they consistently hit quota and how long they’ve been at the company. After some time, they get promoted to lead a "level" within the SDR role itself 

was very surprised something like this worked since some “promotions” have marginal bonus increases, the only thing that changed is their title + the visibility internally at the company
