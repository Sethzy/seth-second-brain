Why doesn’t anyone turn their LinkedIn connections into a pre-qualified outbound list? 

Most sales teams I speak to let it rot.

The play takes 1 afternoon to wire:

1. Download your full LinkedIn connections list.
2. Run the file through Clay for qualification against your current ICP.
3. Enrich company and contact data with Findymail.
4. Reach out with segmented campaigns through HeyReach.

The follow-up does NOT feel cold because there's a real reason you're already connected.

Low hanging fruit.
