## Verbatim Text

If you hear “another vendor is X% cheaper” in sales calls

here’s a good framework you can use to answer:

“I’m familiar with their product, and I don’t want to say anything bad about competitors. What I’ve seen is that customers who switch to us really love features A and B”

then slow the conversation down.

go through your core benefits one by one:
=> does their quote include this?
=> is that important to you?

the goal is to understand where the price difference comes from before touching your discount

ask:

“if price wasn’t the issue, who would you choose?”

only at the end:

“send me their quote. if we’re truly comparable, I’ll see if I can get management to match or beat it. if I can, is there any reason you wouldn’t go with us?”
