## Verbatim Text

5 WhatsApp templates with 25-40% reply rates.

(nobody talks about #4)

1️⃣ They booked a call but might ghost

"Hey [Name]! Just confirming our chat tomorrow at 3pm. Excited to show you [value point, e.g., you can fill your pipeline faster]. That time still works, right?”

Goal: reduce no-shows with a low-pressure nudge.

Don't pitch. Just remind and reassure.

2️⃣ They no-showed

"Hey [Name], guessing things got busy yesterday? 
Anyway, no worries, happens to the best of us.
Would tomorrow or the dat after work better to reschedule?"

Don't guilt-trip them. Open the door back up.

3️⃣ They went quiet after the demo

“Hey [name]! You mentioned [relevant thing they mentioned on the call], no? 
Thought you might find this [relevant resource/insight] helpful: [link]

Is [what you talked about on the demo] still relevant?”

Mention the last interaction.
Add new value. Keep it light.

4️⃣ They changed jobs

"Big congrats on the new role at [Company], [Name]!
We did some great work together at [Previous Company].
Curious what you're focused on now - think there's a chance we could help again?"

This is genuinely one of the highest-converting plays out there.

New company. Fresh budget. Warm relationship.

But only works if you ended things well.

5️⃣ They went cold months ago

"Hey [Name]! We spoke a few months ago about [topic].
Just wanted to let you know we've since [new development].
Thought it might be relevant since [why it’s relevant to them].

Would it make sense to catch up?"

Reference the past conversation.
Highlight what's new.
Keep the ask completely low-pressure.

Most people fail with WhatsApp for one reason:

They copy-paste the same message to everyone.

The app will block you for that.

A few quick tips:

1. Aim for 25-40% response rate to stay safe.
2. Make it relevant to them - personalisation goes a long way.
3. Start at 3-5 messages per day; increase by 10-15% weekly.
4. 2-3 WhatsApp steps per sequence.
5. Text only until trust is built - no links, no PDFs in the first message.

Are you already using WhatsApp for lead generation?
