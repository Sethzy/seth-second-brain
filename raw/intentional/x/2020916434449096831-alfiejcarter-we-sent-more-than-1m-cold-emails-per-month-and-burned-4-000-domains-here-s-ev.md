## Verbatim Text

We sent more than 1M cold emails per month and burned 4,000 domains.

Here’s EVERYTHING I learned about deliverability in 2026:

Most “deliverability advice” comes from people sending 5-50k emails total.

At volume, the rules change.

Deliverability isn’t about spam words or copy.

It’s about infrastructure:

→ How many domains you have.
→ How many inboxes each domain has.
→ Your inbox configuration (SPF, DKIM and DMARC).
→ How much of your volume is sending from Google vs Outlook inboxes.
→ How you choose your inbox providers (if any).
→ How many emails each inbox sends per day.
→ How fast you increase volume per inbox.
→ How you randomize your copy to 3x replies. 
→ Which piece of data you look at when results drop.
→ How you decide what to kill and what to keep.

I packaged all of this into a Cold Email Deliverability Playbook that’s relevant right now in 2026.

This is by far the most important topic in cold email right now. Without this, nothing matters. 

I initially put this together for my team but I decided to make it public.
