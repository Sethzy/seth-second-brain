## Verbatim Text

best advice we got from YC when we started selling our product was to never pretend to be a big company:

A lot of founders think that acting big or giving the impression that they already serve hundreds of customers will help them win their first users

early on, being small is your advantage: reply to messages within 10 minutes, show customers that you can ship features fast & adapt the product specifically for them, something larger companies can't do so quickly

use everything that comes with being a small startup to your advantage, this is how you earn trust and land your first customers if you are still early on

customers want to go on a journey with you! invite them aboard
