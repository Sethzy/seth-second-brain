## Verbatim Text

This 8-part sales deck structure got us to 1,500,000 ARR:

1. Headline & Logos

2. Social proof

3. Use case overview

4. Status quo

5. USP

6. Offer

7. Timeline & deliverables

8. Packages

9. Get started

Here's the breakdown:

1. Headline & Logos

• State your core value prop and set the tone.
• Show both logos (yours + theirs) to signal alignment.

2. Social Proof

• Display logos of clients you’ve worked with.
• Include relevant certifications and awards.

3. Use Case Overview

• Show a graph with the exact areas you help with.
• Clear up which functions you support or streamline.

4. Status Quo

• Show a graph with their current (inefficient) state
• Contrast it with the improved state after working with you.

5. Unique Selling Proposition

• Clearly explain what makes your approach unique, and how you stand out in the market.

6. Offer

• Summarize your product or service, how it works, and the outcome it creates.
• Use plain, jargon-free language.

7. Timeline & Deliverables

Break down:

• what happens
• when
• what the client gets at each step.

8. Packages

• Show pricing tiers or packages with clear differences in scope or depth.
• Provide discount options for upfront payments.
