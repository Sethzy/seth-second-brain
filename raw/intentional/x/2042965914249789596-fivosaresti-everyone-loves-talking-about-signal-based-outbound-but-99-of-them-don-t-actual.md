But 99% of them don’t actually work. 

Because nobody talks about what happens AFTER you capture the signal:

• How do you route it to your best reps?
• Do you send a Slack message or a HubSpot task?
• What's your disqualification logic?
• How do you split territory?

There is no one-size-fits-all, but this is worth looking into.
