## Verbatim Text

If we learned one thing about sales during YC, it's this:

do whatever it takes to onboard the most credible company in your industry as early as possible

during our batch, we built a Company and People Search API to power platforms building sales, recruiting, and investment agents

we spent almost all our time trying to close the top 3 platforms in each category. the whole team flew out to meet their devs. we rebuilt large parts of the product and fixed everything they thought of

once we signed those contracts, every other deal felt 10x easier.

advice for startups: stop holding yourself back because you assume you are not big enough to work with them
