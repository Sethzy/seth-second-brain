Clean design + high information density = best performance.

"I just learned something crazy..." doesn't work.

"We analyzed 47,000 LinkedIn posts.

Not because LinkedIn favors it.

More eyeballs in the first hour = more initial engagement = wider reach.

Tag someone relevant.
Ask a follow-up question.
Add context you couldn't fit in the post.

Starts the comment thread early, 
signals engagement to the algorithm.

Your audience changes.
Your followers forget.

Plus, most people didn't see your best post from 3 months ago.

New hook, same framework, updated examples.

Experts combine the two in one.

→ Questions outperform statements

→ "Here's what we learned from X" outperforms "Here's what I think about X"
