## Verbatim Text

the goal in sales discovery is to quantify the pain:

layer 1:
propsect: "we need to find a better way to manage our team's follow-ups. It’s a mess"

answer: "I hear that a lot, but help me understand, is "messy" just annoying for the team, or is it actually hurting your numbers?

layer 2:
prospect: "both. we’re seeing a lot of leads go dark after the initial demo

"answer: "okay, let's look at the math. if you had to estimate, how many of those "dark" leads should have moved to a second call last month?"

layer 3:
prospect: "probably 15 or 20"

answer: "and with your average deal size being $10k, we're talking about $150k to $200k in pipeline, is that correct?"

and you can (optionally) continue going further with "how much time do we have to solve this problem? can you afford losing that in the next quarter too?"

always ask more questions. the goal is to understand the pain behind the surface answer
