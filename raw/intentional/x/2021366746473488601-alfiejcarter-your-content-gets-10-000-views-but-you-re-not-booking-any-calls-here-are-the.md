## Verbatim Text

Your content gets 10,000 views but you're not booking ANY calls.

Here are the 3 most common reasons (and fast fixes):

1. You're posting about everything

❌ Problem: Nobody knows what you actually do.
✅ Fix: Pick one core outcome and own it.

Example: Don't post about "LinkedIn outreach" one day and "personal productivity" the next.

Post about LinkedIn strategy so many times that people start associating LinkedIn outreach with your name. 

2. You're using the same format for every stage

❌ Problem: Early-stage leads need education. Late-stage leads need proof.
✅ Fix: Match format to lead stage.

Top of funnel: Frameworks, how-to content, workflows.

Bottom of funnel: Case studies, specific metrics, process breakdowns

3. You never mention what you actually offer

❌ Problem: People don't know how to buy from you.
✅ Fix: Every 3-4 posts, make the CTA about your service.

"We do this for agencies. If you want the same system, DM me 'pipeline'."

Views don't matter if they don't convert.
