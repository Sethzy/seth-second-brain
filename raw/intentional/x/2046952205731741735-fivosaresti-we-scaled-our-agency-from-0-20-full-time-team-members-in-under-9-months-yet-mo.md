We scaled our agency from 0→ 20 full-time team members in under 9 months.

Yet, most agencies die from the inside because of this: 

Team chaos that NO ONE addresses.

I watched this happen at companies I worked at before starting Workflows. 

> Talented people with zero structure. 
> Feedback that felt personal instead of objective. 
> Zero clarity on what "doing well" actually meant.

So when we started hiring, we went straight to the source... @Dan__rosenthal and I studied how BCG runs talent.

Then I rebuilt it for an agency our size.

The result is a scoring system we now run 3x a year across our entire 20-person team.

1. Every role has 4 core competencies. 
2. Each is scored 0.5 to 4. 
3. Your composite score puts you at P1 through P4… and that determines your bonus, your promotion, and your trajectory at the company.

One of our strongest technical guys had been ignoring feedback for months. 

The moment we showed him his scores objectively laid out, something clicked. 

He started improving almost immediately.

That's what structure does.
