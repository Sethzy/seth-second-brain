## Verbatim Text

If I had to close 5 new customers ASAP, I wouldn’t send a single cold email asking for a zoom meeting:

1) find the nearest event or conference
2) get the attendee list
3) email every relevant prospect:

“hey X, I saw you’re also attending Y on Tuesday.

I’ll be there too. Would you be down to grab a 10-minute coffee to connect?”

then hold back-to-back meetings and talk to as many relevant people as possible.

It’s 10x harder to say “no” to a handshake than to an email
