## Verbatim Text

STOP posting video clips on LinkedIn.

A static infographic can 10x the performance of a post.

Almost every one of our posts has a visual. 

Here is what we learned from 44 posts last month:

1. LinkedIn rewards dwell time
- A static infographic keeps people reading.
- A video clip auto-scrolls past.

2. Every post gets a visual
- Almost every post we publish has a custom Figma design.
 - Our design system lives in Figma with reusable templates.

3. The ROI is asymmetric
- One infographic template can be reused across 10+ posts.
- One video takes hours of editing for a single use.

Tools we use: 

- Figma
- Miro
- Screen Studio.

Test a static infographic on your next post.

I can almost guarantee it performs better.
