# Why connect Claude to LinkedIn?

If you’re doing LinkedIn outreach at scale, the process is almost always the same: you search for leads manually, build lists based on incomplete information, guess who might be interested, write messages one by one, send them, and then wait without really knowing what is working or not. By the time you start to understand patterns or identify what resonates, the moment of intent has often already passed and the opportunity is lost.

Claude fundamentally changes this dynamic by collapsing the entire loop into a continuous, data-driven system. Instead of manually defining every step, you simply describe your ideal customer. From there, Claude identifies high-intent prospects, enriches them with real buying signals such as job changes, hiring activity, or engagement patterns, and initiates highly personalized conversations directly on LinkedIn.

The system does not stop at execution. It continuously analyzes performance, refines targeting based on real responses, updates intent signals, and surfaces clear insights on what to improve next. Over time, this creates a compounding effect where both targeting and messaging become increasingly precise without requiring additional manual effort.

There are no spreadsheets to maintain, no lists to constantly rebuild, and no need to manually manage outreach or follow-ups. Instead of operating a fragmented process, you are running a unified system that handles discovery, personalization, execution, and optimization in one place.

This guide walks through the full setup in just a few minutes, the first prompts to run to get immediate results, and the exact workflows that allow you to consistently find, convert, and scale high-intent leads on LinkedIn.

From there, we launch and orchestrate outreach campaigns at scale.

## Step 1 — Open Claude in your browser

Go to claude(dot)ai.  Don't use the Claude desktop app for this — custom connectors only work in the web version right now.

## Step 2— Open Settings → Connectors

Click your profile icon in the bottom left corner, open Settings, and switch to the Connectors tab. Scroll to the bottom of the connector list and click "Add custom connector."

## Step 3 — Paste your Gojiberry MCP server URL

Name the connector "Gojiberry AI" (or anything you want). Paste this link into the URL field :

```
https://mcp.gojiberry.ai

```

Click on "Connect"

Add your Gojiberry API Key (You need a free GojiberryAI account for that)

You can find it here on your Account: in settings -> API.Then add it on the previous screenshot.

Enable Gojiberry in your conversation

Once added, enable the connector per conversation. Click the + button in the chat input (bottom-left), open Connectors, and toggle Gojiberry AI on.

## Step 4 — Let's find some leads !

Open a new chat in claude(dot)ai. You're live. Claude now has direct read-and-write access to your Gojiberry account.

A) Ask Claude to find you some leads

B) Get the leads

C) Preview your leads in GojiberryAI, then ask Claude to enrich each profile and generate a highly personalized message based on real-time intent signals and online activity.

D) Launch an AI crafted campaign in one click within Gojiberry

E) Get crazy reply and connections rates
