## Verbatim Text

Most teams run 1 (maybe 2) outbound campaign types.

Here are 6 you should be running at all times:

1. Competitors

• Find out which companies are using competitors
• Pitch the switch with a direct comparison
• Tool: BuiltWith, PredictLeads, TheirStack

2. Case Study

• Pull every closed-won case study you have
• Find lookalike companies by firmographics (you can use ocean(.)io)
• Match the case study to the audience you send it to

3. Web Visitors

• De-anonymize website traffic with Warmly(.)io
• Route to Clay, auto-qualify, personalize based on the page they visited
• Reach out the same day the visit happens

4. Signals and Triggers

• Hiring, funding, job changes, tech stack shifts
• Score each signal by recency and relevance
• Route hot signals to phone-first outreach

5. Evergreen

• Social engagement, champion-follows, job change alerts
• Runs with zero manual effort once plumbed
• Tool: Warmly, Trigify, CommonRoom, Clay

6. Macro

• Your general ICP list, well-written copy, no signal
• This is the base of your volume
• Attach a lead magnet on the breakup email

You'll make most of your money on the cold campaigns. But don't ignore these signal-based auto plays.

Running all 6 is the reason 1 GTM engineer and 1 rep can outwork a 6-person SDR team on signal coverage alone.
