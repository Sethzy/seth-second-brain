I run GTM for dozens of $10-$15M companies. 

Trust me when I tell you...

Cold outbound is still one of the most effective B2B marketing channels. 

Here’s our 5-stage process for getting results with it 2026:

(This ENTIRE motion can run with 1 GTM engineer and 1 sales rep)

Stage 1: ICP Model & Campaign Strategy

Define who you're going after:

Map out all different segments by:
- Firmographics
- Technographics
- Account fit signals

Pick your campaign type:
- Volume-based
- Micro campaigns
- Signal-based

Select your plays e.g.:
- Website visitor retargeting
- Ad engagement
- Closed-lost reopens

The plays you wanna run dictate the tools you need to get.

Stage 2: Account Sourcing & Stakeholder Mapping

3 methods to build your lists:
- Databases: Apollo, https://t.co/1LS8EVquLW, DiscoLike
- Scraping: Apify, Serper, Python
- Signals: Findymail, RB2B, Jungler, Fibbler, Sumble

Route data to clay:
- Dedupe and normalize data
- Qualify companies with custom agents using ChatGPT
- Source contacts with AI Ark and BetterContact

Step 3: Copywriting

Your message has 3 pillars:
- Positioning: why you, why now
- Offer: what's in it for them
- Personalization: make it specific

Sequences should have multiple versions per segment:
- A/B test 4 variants at each step and let data pick the winner
- LinkedIn messages should be shorter and more conversational

Stage 4: Campaign Execution

Add your leads list to:
- https://t.co/MNNjjMohQr for email sequences
- https://t.co/jxwmf3ph0v for LinkedIn sequences

Configure limits properly - deliverability is everything:
- 15 emails per day per mailbox
- 20 DMs per day per LinkedIn account

Reverse engineer numbers based on your contact list to decide how many LinkedIn and email accounts you need.

Stage 5: CRM Sync & Reporting

OutboundSync x https://t.co/MNNjjMohQr x https://t.co/jxwmf3ph0v:
- Log all activities to CRM and create reporting
- Call tasks on Nooks auto-created for reps
- Slack notifications on positive replies with phone number data using BetterContact

This ENTIRE motion can run with 1 GTM engineer and 1 sales rep.

Even if you're not ready to hire an outbound team, you should have an automated engine running.
