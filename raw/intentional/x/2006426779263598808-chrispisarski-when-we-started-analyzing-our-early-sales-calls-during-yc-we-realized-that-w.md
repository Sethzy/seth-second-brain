## Verbatim Text

when we started analyzing our early sales calls during YC, we realized that we were constantly asking “does that make sense?”

the problem with that is that prospects always tend to say yes, so we replaced that question with this:

“you mentioned earlier that [Problem X] was a priority. How does this feature compare to how you are doing that process right now?”

you make the prospect explain your value back to you. And if they can’t articulate the difference, you know that they are still confused about how they can use your product, which you can fix right then instead of losing the deal at the end
