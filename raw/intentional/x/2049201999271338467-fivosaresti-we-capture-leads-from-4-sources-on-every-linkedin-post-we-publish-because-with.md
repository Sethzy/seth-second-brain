We capture leads from 4 sources on every LinkedIn post we publish.
 
Because without a capture system, none of them reach your pipeline.

1. Lead magnet comments, captured via Tally
2. Website visitors, identified by RB2B
3. Profile viewers, tracked by Teamfluence
4. Post engagement, monitored through Jungler

→ Every lead streams into Clay.
→ Each one gets enriched and cross-checked against HubSpot.
→ Tier 1 leads get a Slack notification and a task for 1:1 outreach with Apollo.
→ The rest go into automated sequences: InstantlyAI for email, HeyReach for LinkedIn.

Content is the top of the pipeline, and everything after it runs automatically.
