## Verbatim Text

In the last 12 months, I’ve generated well over 5M impressions through LinkedIn. 

The biggest misconception about content is that the goal is awareness.

• Post something
• Get seen
• Maybe do a promotion.

But the actual goal of content is solving your ICP's problems for free.

Because if you solve their problems through content, you don't need a crazy sales process.

They already trust you.

That said...

This is the 70/20/10 split we follow to generate 75-85% of our pipeline through content: 

• 70% educational.
• 20% frameworks and how-tos.
• 10% case studies and offers.

The 70% builds the pipeline.

The 10% just captures it.
