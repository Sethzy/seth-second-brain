## Verbatim Text

stop attacking competitors in sales calls

a useful sales advice we got during YC was to ask this question if a prospect mentions their current provider:

“what would you rate them out of 10?”

if they respond with 8, follow up with

 “that’s pretty high, what would need to change exactly to make it a 9?”

they will start listing the gaps they currently have with your competitor, and you should take over from that point , not before this question
