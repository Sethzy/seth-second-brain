## Verbatim Text

The most underrated question in a sales cycle: "Who would feel left out if they weren’t apart of this evaluation?"

Not "is there anyone else I should talk to?" That makes it sounds like it’s all about you.

Best way to do it is to open ups Mutual Success Plan on a screenshare and walk through it together.

Legal review. Security review. Finance approval. IT onboarding. Change management.

And then saying: "For the legal and security rows - who owns that process? I want to make sure we're giving them enough runway."

𝗧𝗵𝗮𝘁 𝗾𝘂𝗲𝘀𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗱𝗼𝗲𝘀 𝗳𝗼𝘂𝗿 𝘁𝗵𝗶𝗻𝗴𝘀 𝗮𝘁 𝗼𝗻𝗰𝗲.

→ It positions you as someone who's done this before.

→ It gets the right stakeholders surfaced without it feeling like you're fishing.

→ It moves legal and security from a last-minute fire drill to a planned milestone.

→ And it naturally expands your map of the org.

The screenshare makes all the difference.

When those line items are already on a shared doc, the conversation is collaborative.

When you bring it up F2F on a Zoom call, it can feel like an interrogation.

I built a free MSP template: https://t.co/DUR060M4zl

Go get her.
