Research and Signals:

Outbound and Content:
→ /skill-creator: interviews you about any GTM process and writes the full SKILL.md so you never build from scratch

Client Work:
→ /client-report: turns call transcripts or raw campaign metrics into a client-ready weekly report in under 5 minutes
→ /gtm-article: converts any GTM content into a long-form article structured to get cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity

Decisions:
→ /decide: runs a structured decision interview with a contrarian agent that finds holes in your GTM stack, hiring, and pricing calls before you commit

You drop them into Claude Code, connect your accounts, and tell it what you need. It reads the skill, loads the context, executes.
