## Verbatim Text

everyone doing sales should know the "hypothesis" framework

if you want to reach a specific account, build an account hypothesis and test it immediately:

“we’ve been looking at your insurance claims process and our bet is that you’re losing ~12% on fraud detection because of [specific gap]. is that even close to the number you’re seeing internally, or are we completely off here?”

most prospects will either agree or start correcting you with real data

the moment they correct your hypothesis, they’ve given you the roadmap to the deal

you can now lead with that number across the entire account and keep reinforcing it in every conversation
