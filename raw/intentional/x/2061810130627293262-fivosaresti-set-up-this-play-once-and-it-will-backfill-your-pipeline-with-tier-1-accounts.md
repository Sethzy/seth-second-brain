Set up this play once and it will backfill your pipeline with Tier 1 accounts EVERY quarter.

Because “Customer alumni” are the warmest leads in B2B.

When someone moves from a company that already bought your product to a new company, that new company becomes a strong target with a built-in champion already inside.

The play that surfaces them runs in 5 steps:

1) Pull closed-won accounts from HubSpot
→ Every account that paid you in the last 24 months is a seed for alumni movement.

2) Find former employees who moved through Clay
→ Clay matches former employee data against current LinkedIn data and surfaces the job changes.

3) Qualify their current company against your ICP inside Clay
→ Run firmographic filters on size, industry, and tech stack so non-ICP movers drop out before scoring.

4) Score each company with ChatGPT inside Clay
→ Assign Tier 1, 2, or 3 based on fit, signal strength, and timing.

5) Route outreach by tier
→ Tier 1 accounts get manual prospecting inside HubSpot.
→ Tier 2 gets automated email through Instantly and LinkedIn DMs through HeyReach.
→ Tier 3 gets automated email through Instantly only.

This works because the champion already knows your product.

They already lived with it inside their last company.

The opener references the previous deployment specifically, with no generic intro line.

Reply rates on a sourced alumni list run higher than any cold list of equivalent size.
