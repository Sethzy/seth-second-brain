## Verbatim Text

Everyone says “add value” between calls but nobody tells you how.

Here are my go-to plays for value touches:

1. Review your last call summary and ask yourself:

· What rapport did we build? (Sports teams, vacation plans, kids, hobbies)

· What business challenges did they mention?

· What questions did they ask that I didn't fully answer?

· What seemed to resonate most with them?

· What concerns or objections came up?

2. Check Their Digital Footprint

· LinkedIn Activity: What are they posting about? What are they engaging with?

· Company LinkedIn: Recent announcements, funding, leadership changes

· Google News: Search "[Company Name] + news" for recent coverage

· Industry Publications: What's trending in their space?

· Their Company Blog: Recent posts, thought leadership pieces

An example:

"Hey Sarah,

Great talking last week! Re-listened to our call and know you were curious how we helped with churn mitigation.

Sharing a case study here of how Acme used gong to reduce churn by 23% YoY.

Excited to get more into it on Friday.

A few keys:

-Short and sweet

-Be specific - ensure this isn’t some template

-Don’t give them homework to do (sending questions)

Any other go-to value touches?
