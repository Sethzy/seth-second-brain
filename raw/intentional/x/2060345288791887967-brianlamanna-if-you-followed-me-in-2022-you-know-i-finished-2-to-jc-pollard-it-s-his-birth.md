-Leadership is only 50% a sales job. Do you like hiring? Recruiting? Leading people?

-What fires you up more… closing a big deal? Or seeing a rep go from average to Club?

It really comes down to what in your day gives you energy vs. what burns you out. 

Do more of what gives you energy.
