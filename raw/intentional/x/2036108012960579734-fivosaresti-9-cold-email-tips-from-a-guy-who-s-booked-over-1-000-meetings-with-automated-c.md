9 cold email tips from a guy who’s booked over 1,000+ meetings with automated cold outbound: 

> Disable tracking links
> 2 mailboxes per domain
> 50/50 Google and Outlook
> Warm up for 2+ weeks minimum
> Buy separate domains for outreach
> Do not skip SPF, DKIM, and DMARC set up
> Max 20 emails per day per mailbox
> Use spintax in ALL your copy
> Verify every email address

As cold outbound gets more difficult by the day...

These have never been more important. 

Bookmark this.
