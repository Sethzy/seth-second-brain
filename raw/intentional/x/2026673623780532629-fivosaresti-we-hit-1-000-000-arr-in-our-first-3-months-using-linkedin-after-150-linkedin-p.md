We hit $1,000,000 ARR in our first 3 months using LinkedIn.

After 150+ LinkedIn posts...

I’m convinced that 80% of post success comes from 2 things:

1. Hook
2. Visual

The hook stops the scroll.

If your first line doesn't make someone pause, the rest doesn't matter.

Most people bury their best idea in paragraph 3.

By then, nobody's reading.

The visual makes it shareable.

LinkedIn is a visual platform now.

Plain text posts get scrolled past.

But a well-designed visual:

• Stops the scroll
• Gets saved and shared
• Makes the post memorable
