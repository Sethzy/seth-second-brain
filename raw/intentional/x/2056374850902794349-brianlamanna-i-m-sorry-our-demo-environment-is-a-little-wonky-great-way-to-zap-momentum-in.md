“I’m sorry - our demo environment is a little wonky.”

Great way to zap momentum in a call.

Every org has buggy / outdated demo spaces.

Blaming it on the environment is lazy.

𝗛𝗼𝘄 𝘁𝗼 𝗮𝘃𝗼𝗶𝗱 𝟵𝟱% 𝗼𝗳 𝗶𝘀𝘀𝘂𝗲𝘀:

-Write out the exact click path in notes
-Walk through the click path, start-to-finish
-Pre-load a few screens/pages on to other tabs
-Never click anywhere, that you don’t know what it’ll show

And if you REALLY need to click somewhere you didn’t prepare.

Pause your screen share and test it out real quickly.

Definition of control what you can control 💯
