## Verbatim Text

when a sales prospect asks: "does your tool integrate with Salesforce?"

a lot of people get excited and immediately reply: "yes it does!"

Instead of doing that, try to understand the intent behind the question and learn more about the prospect

for example:
"it does. but I’m curious, how are you using Salesforce today that makes this integration so important for you?"

most of the time, this leads them to reveal a pain point they hadn’t mentioned before and you can always tie the conversation back to the problem your integration is actually solving during the call
