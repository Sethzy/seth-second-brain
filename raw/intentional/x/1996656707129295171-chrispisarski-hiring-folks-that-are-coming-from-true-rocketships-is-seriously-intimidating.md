## Verbatim Text

Hiring folks that are coming from true rocketships is seriously intimidating.

They know what ‘amazing’ looks like. You are being compared to a top 0.001% founder and have to live up to that standard every day - or grow into it fast.

But, there are crazy upsides:

They raise expectations.

They can bring folks that scaled other departments.

They upgrade the way you think, ship, plan, recruit and talk to customers.

It's great to just have these teammates close by and hear how their previous company/founder handled any situation you are coming up against.
