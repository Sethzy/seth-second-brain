## Verbatim Text

if you are trying to multi-thread a B2B sales deal, consider using a framing question like this to bring more people into the conversation:

“typically, deals work best when we achieve X, Y, and Z. t hat usually involves Person 1 and Person 2 on your side, and Person 3 on ours. I want to make sure you’re comfortable looping in those stakeholders so I can bring in the right people from my team to move this forward. How does that sound?”

this approach is much more effective than just asking,
“who would feel left out if they weren’t on the next call?”
