## Verbatim Text

The modern GTM stack rewrites itself every 3-4 years, and revenue teams pick up new job titles each time.

Five tools, five eras:

2010-2014: Foundation
→ Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo
CRMs become table stakes.

2015-2016: Sales Engagement
→ Outreach, Salesloft
Sequences default the outbound motion. SDR role explodes.

2017-2019: Revenue Intelligence
→ Gong, Clari, ZoomInfo
Call recordings and enrichment drive rep prioritization.

2020-2022: Intent Data
→ Bombora, UserGems, Demandbase
Signal tracking goes mainstream. ABM hits the agenda.

2023-2024: Workflow Automation
→ Clay, n8n, Common Room
Orchestration becomes the moat. 'GTM Engineer' becomes a real job title.

2025-2026+: Agentic GTM
→ Claude Code, Nooks, Seam AI, Ergo
Decisions get outsourced to agents. MCPs and CLIs replace UIs.

The next title on the org chart is being written this year.
