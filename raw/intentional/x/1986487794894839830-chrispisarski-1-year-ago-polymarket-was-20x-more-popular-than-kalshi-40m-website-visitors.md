## Verbatim Text

1 year ago - Polymarket was 20x more popular than Kalshi. 
40M website visitors vs only 2M.

Now, Kalshi has surpassed them in trade volume.

What happened?

We looked at both platforms’ web traffic and traded volumes over the last year. The momentum is shifting rapidly towards Kalshi.

1) Traded volumes (then vs now)

Kalshi’s liquidity curve steepened dramatically and, in October, it edged past Polymarket on trade volume.

- September 2024 - Kalshi: $21M vs Polymarket: $533M
- October 2025 - Kalshi: $4.4B vs Polymarket: $4.0B

2) Popularity & awareness (web traffic)

The traffic gap narrowed by ~10M visits YoY. Polymarket still leads by a substantial amount but has lost a lot of traffic and Kalshi’s closing in.

- Sept 2024: Kalshi 0.2M vs Polymarket 16.0M visits
- Sept 2025: Kalshi 2.6M vs Polymarket 9.3M visits

Kalshi has clearly seized momentum and has surpassed Polymarket.

Main reasons:

1. Clever, more viral marketing, e.g. Kalashi’s AI superbowl ad.
2. Kalshi can operate in the US, Polymarket cannot.
3. Kalshi is attracting professional trading firms and funds, increasing liquidity and making their markets more attractive.

Polymarket’s U.S. re-entry later this month and token plans both could re-accelerate user growth and challenge Kalshi’s growth.

The prediction market space is up for grabs - it’ll be interesting to see who wins the prediction market space.
