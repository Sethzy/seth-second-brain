## Verbatim Text

Is YC really not accepting Indian founders anymore? 

A post went viral yesterday claiming exactly that.

So we checked the data using the CrustData API, which tracks the background and location of every YC founder and startup by batch.

Turns out, that’s not true.

Yes, there was a drop after 2021 - but the share of YC startups with at least one Indian founder has actually started going up.

In 2020, around 12% of all YC startups had an Indian founder.  It dropped to 8% in 2023, and is now climbing back above 10%.

That means over 1 in 10 YC (!) startups today have at least one Indian-origin founder.
