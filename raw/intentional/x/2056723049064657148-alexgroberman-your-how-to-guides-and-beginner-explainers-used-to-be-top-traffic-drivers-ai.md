Your how-to guides and beginner explainers used to be top traffic drivers.

AI is now answering those questions for buyers before they ever click through to your site.

The traffic is not coming back, but there is a way to make this work in your favor.

Let's discuss:

According to the same research, the content that earned sustained AI citations shared a consistent profile: original data, expert voice, and clear structure that AI platforms could extract and attribute.

Your top-of-funnel content needs to get cited by AI platforms to drive awareness. Your bottom-funnel content needs to convert the buyers AI sends to you. The brands winning in 2026 are doing both.

If your brand's content is one of the sources the AI cites, you get two things: brand visibility (the buyer sees your brand mentioned as a credible source) and click-through traffic from the small percentage of buyers who want to go deeper.
