## Verbatim Text

We've set up dozens of ABM programs.

This is the best methodology we've found to ACTUALLY get reps to use RevOps systems.

Awareness scoring. 

There are five stages, all auto-updated by workflows in your CRM:

1. Identified
2. Aware
3. Interested
4. Evaluating
5. Selecting

The first and last are fixed:
→ Identified = on your TAL, no engagement yet
→ Selecting = open deal in your CRM

The middle three are yours to customize.

Variables worth considering:
• Website visit in the last 30 days
• Newsletter signup
• Repeat website visits
• Content download
• Webinar attendance
• High-intent page visit (pricing, comparison)

For each signal you set 3 things:
→ Recency (e.g. last 30 days)
→ Threshold (e.g. 3+ opens, 50+ impressions, 2+ clicks)
→ Stage priority (higher stage wins on overlap)

Accounts move both ways. If activity drops below threshold, the account falls back a stage.

Most common tools to feed this: Fibbler, Jungler, HubSpot, Clay.

Unlike most things RevOps ships, reps LOVE this one.
