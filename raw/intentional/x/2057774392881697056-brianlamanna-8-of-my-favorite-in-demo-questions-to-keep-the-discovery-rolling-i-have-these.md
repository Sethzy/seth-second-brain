## Verbatim Text

8 of my favorite in-demo questions to keep the discovery 🚂 rolling.

I have these in front of me on every presentation.

1. “What else comes to your mind when you see this in how you would leverage it?”

2. “What would this allow your team to do differently?”

3. “How important are these insights for you to have?”

4. “Tell me about your current process for this today?”

5. “How would you do this without *this feature*”

6. “What are the ripple effects of this challenge on the business?”

7. "How important is this problem to the organization?"

8. “How does this compare to what you are doing today?”

Be intentional with asking strong, open-ended questions

And ditch the "umm so... any questions so far?"

Keep discovery going, keep the deal going.
