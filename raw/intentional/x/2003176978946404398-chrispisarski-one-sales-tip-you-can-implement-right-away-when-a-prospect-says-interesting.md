One sales tip you can implement right away:

when a prospect says: “Interesting, let’s circle back in 3 months,”

don’t just say, “sure I’ll reach out then”

say this instead:

“awesome. So we don’t have to play email tag in March, how does the 15th sound? I’ll send you a 15-minute placeholder on the calendar

If the timing is off or something comes up, you can always reschedule.”
