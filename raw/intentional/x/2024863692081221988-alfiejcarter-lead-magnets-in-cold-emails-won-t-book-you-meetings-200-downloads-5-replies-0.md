Lead magnets in cold emails won’t book you meetings.

200 downloads. 5 replies. 0 calls booked.

Sound familiar?

But the problem isn't the lead magnet - it's everything that happens after you send it.

Here's what's actually broken:

→ No qualification built in - anyone can opt in, which means your list is full of collectors

→ No tracking - you send the link and have zero idea who actually read it

→ No follow-up tied to engagement - everyone gets the same sequence regardless of how they behaved

Here's what to do instead:

1️⃣ Pick a topic that filters while it teaches - "Signal-Based List Building" only attracts agencies running outbound at scale. "How to Get More Leads" attracts everyone and converts nobody.

2️⃣ Structure it in 3 parts - a quick win they can implement in under 10 minutes, the full system with exact tools and screenshots, then how to scale it. That third section is where you make your service feel obvious.

3️⃣ Host it in Notion - so you can track time on page and section engagement. Someone who spends 8 minutes on the "automation setup" section is telling you exactly what they want help with.

4️⃣ Build a 3-email sequence around it - Day 0: offer it. Day 0 again: deliver it the second they reply. Day 4: a case study from a similar company. That's it.

5️⃣ Follow up based on what they actually did - not just that they downloaded it.

I broke down the whole system - step by step, with copy templates and the exact delivery setup in my free playbook.
