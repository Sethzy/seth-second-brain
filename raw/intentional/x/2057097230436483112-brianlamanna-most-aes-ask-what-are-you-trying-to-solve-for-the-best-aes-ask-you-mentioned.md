## Verbatim Text

Most AEs ask: "What are you trying to solve for?"

The best AEs ask: "You mentioned that's the #1 priority… how did you determine that?"

One question goes surface level. 

The other exposes the real problem, internal politics, and urgency.

𝗔 𝗳𝗲𝘄 𝗼𝗳 𝗺𝘆 𝗳𝗮𝘃𝗼𝗿𝗶𝘁𝗲𝘀:

→ "What metric is that most affecting today — and where are you trying to get it?”

→ "Have you done the math on what solving this would mean to the business?”

→ "Fast forward 90 days - are we okay if we haven't addressed this, or are we committed to solving it yesterday?"

Discovery is about listening, showing genuine curiosity, and asking the follow-up to go deeper.

But these plug and play questions can help open up the door.
