## Verbatim Text

Sales teams keep stacking more tools and the workflow keeps getting slower.

A rep sending one personalized cold email tabs through 5 dashboards before they hit send.

• LinkedIn for the prospect's recent activity.
• HubSpot for the deal history.
• Apollo for the title and company.
• Findymail for the verified email.
• Instantly or HeyReach for the sequence.

That workflow eats roughly 20 minutes per email.

30 reps sending 50 emails a day means most of the team is inside tool tabs before a single reply lands.

Each tool earns its seat.

What's missing is one layer on top of the stack you can talk to.

Claude pulls the LinkedIn activity, the HubSpot history, the Apollo enrichment, the Findymail verification, and pushes the sequence into Instantly in the same prompt.

It is NOT the tool count that’s been costing you. 

The whole time, context-switch has been the problem.
