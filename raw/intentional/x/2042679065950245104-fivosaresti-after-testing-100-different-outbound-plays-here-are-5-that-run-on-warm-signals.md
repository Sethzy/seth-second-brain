After testing 100+ different outbound plays...

Here are 5 that run on WARM signals from existing behavior:

(BOOKMARK THIS)

1. Customer Alumni

- Pull closed-won accounts from your CRM.
- Use Clay to find people who left.
- Qualify their new company against your ICP.
- Call them with Nooks and BetterContact.

These people already know your product works.

2. LinkedIn Connections

- Download your entire connections list.
- Run it through Clay for ICP qualification.
- Enrich contacts with Findymail.
- Launch segmented outreach with HeyReach.

My guess is that you are sitting on hundreds of leads right now.

3. Content Engagement

- Track post engagement with Jungler.
- Route to Clay for scoring.
- Filter by repeat engagement frequency.
- Reach out using HeyReach and Instantly.

4. Keyword Monitoring

- Monitor relevant keywords and brand mentions with Jungler.
- Capture engagement data.
- Send to Clay for enrichment.
- Find decision makers and run automated sequences.

These are people actively talking about problems you solve.

5. Website Visitor De-anonymization

- Connect RB2B pixel to your website.
- Get company and contact level data.
- Route to Clay for qualification.
- Find more decision makers and multi-thread the account.

Signal-based plays get 3-5x the reply rates of standard cold lists.

—

The common thread across all five:

None of them start with a cold list.
