Why is nobody is setting up HubSpot Cards?

Configured properly, your reps get Clay's enrichment without EVER opening Clay.

This is the easiest automation win you can ship this quarter:

Setup:
1. Edit your contact or company view
2. Add a card on the right-side
3. Choose 'Enroll in Workflow' from the card library
4. Set up a workflow sending data to Clay via webhook
5. Send the data back to HubSpot from the Clay workflow

What your reps can pull from a card:
• Phone number → BetterContact
• Email address → Findymail
• HQ location → Apollo(.)io
• Additional stakeholders → Apollo(.)io

Bookmark it.
