## Verbatim Text

Founders are awful at asking for intros.

Every week, I get messages like:

“Hey, can you intro me to X?”

And then they wonder why nothing happens.

When someone introduces you, they put their reputation and time on the line.

Before you ask for an intro, ask yourself:

Does this make the person look good or desperate on my behalf?

A good intro request makes them feel like they’re adding value, not begging for attention.

Example:

“Hey, I’m building Y. 

I think X would genuinely benefit from talking to us because Z. 

If this makes sense, could you intro us? 

Happy to send a short blurb.”

And if they say yes, make it frictionless:

- One line on who you are
- One line on how you know each other
- One line on why the recipient should care
- Zero editing required

That’s how you get more intros.
