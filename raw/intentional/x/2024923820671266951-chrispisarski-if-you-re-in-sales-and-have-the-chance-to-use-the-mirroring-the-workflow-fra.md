## Verbatim Text

If you’re in sales and have the chance to use the "mirroring the workflow" framework, you should do it

we used to say this to some prospects during YC:

“before i show you the platform, could you share your screen and show me exactly how you’re solving [problem] manually right now? i want to make sure i only show you the parts of our tool that actually augment your specific process”

after that, you show them exactly how you can solve and automate this using your product

one of the best magic moments you can create
