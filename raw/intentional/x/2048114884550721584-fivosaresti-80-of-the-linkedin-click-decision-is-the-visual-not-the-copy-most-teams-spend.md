## Verbatim Text

80% of the LinkedIn click decision is the visual, NOT the copy.

Most teams spend all their time writing and almost none on design.

What we did to hit ~140K+ followers across our company:

• Every post gets a custom infographic designed in Figma
• Banner and featured section are treated as conversion assets
• Hook stays under 55 characters so it renders fully on mobile

After the click, the post needs to be relatable and skimmable with a clear point of view.

The click only happens if the visual stops the scroll.
