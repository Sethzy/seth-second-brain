12 Exceptional Questions to Ask on Discovery Calls.

In collab with Sheriff Shahen at Deel.

1. What's happening in the business now, that's driving [X] to be a priority?

2. Have you tried to solve this before? What happened?

3. What trumps that as an even larger initiative this year?

4. Why now? What's different about this year versus last?

5. Silly question — but fast forward 90 days. Are we okay if we haven't addressed this yet? Or are we committed to solving it now?

6. Who's feeling this the most on a day-to-day basis?

7. What's the biggest single risk to making this a success?

8. What does good look like 6 months from now?

9. If you had built this slide, what would you add?

10. Walk me through how you'd make a decision like this.

11. My biggest concern is [X] — is it fair to ask [action]?

12. What happens if you don't fix this?

What should #13 be?
