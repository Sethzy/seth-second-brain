## Verbatim Text

The fastest way to improve your outbound is to email FEWER companies.

We cut 91% of our TAM before sending a single message.

Started with 66,000 companies. 
Ended with 5,700 qualified accounts.

How we did it:

1. Data sourcing
• Combined 5 sources: BuiltWith, Crunchbase, Apollo, GetLatka, and Exa
• All datasets normalized in Clay

2. SaaS verification
• Built an AI research agent to verify each company was actually SaaS
• Agent checked for subscription pricing, product feature pages, and dashboard screenshots
• Non-SaaS companies eliminated automatically

3. ICP scoring
• Model based on company size, location, funding stage, and tech stack
• Clay company enrichment for headcount
• BuiltWith for tech stack, Crunchbase for funding
• Focused on VC-funded companies using HubSpot

4. Tier allocation
• Dream 150: manual AE-led outreach
• Tier 1: cold call and semi-automated
• Tier 2: automated email and LinkedIn
• Tier 3: automated email only

Sending emails to 66,000 companies would have destroyed our deliverability.

The work that happens before the first email matters more than the email itself.
