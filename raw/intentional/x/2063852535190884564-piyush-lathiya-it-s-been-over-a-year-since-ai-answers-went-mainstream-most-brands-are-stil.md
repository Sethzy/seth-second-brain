## Verbatim Text

It’s been over a year since AI answers went mainstream.
Most brands are still optimizing as if it didn’t.

Well, the search is now split into three layers.

And most strategies are still stuck in just one.

#SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
You compete for rankings and clicks.

#AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
You compete to become the direct answer.

#GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
You compete to be cited inside AI-generated responses.

That last shift is where most brands lose.

AI selects a few sources and builds the answer around them.

Which means:
• Ranking doesn’t guarantee visibility.
• Answering doesn’t guarantee selection.
• And most content isn’t built to be extracted or trusted.

This is why “Search Everywhere” is the real strategy now.

Not SEO vs AEO vs GEO.

But all three are working together.
• SEO gets you discovered
• AEO gets you surfaced
• GEO gets you cited

Miss one, and you disappear where it matters most.

P.S. If you don’t know where your brand shows up in AI answers, you’re guessing. We built @trackvisibility to track prompts, citations, and gaps across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and more.
