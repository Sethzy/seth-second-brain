## Verbatim Text

never end a sales call without the next one on the calendar.

this is what we learned during our time at YC:

founder: "great I'll email you the proposal later today!"
Prospect: "Sounds good, I'll take a look."
*you chase them for 2 weeks and they ghost you *

If you leave the next step up to the prospect, life often get in the way and you will fall to the bottom of their priority list.

Say this instead:

"I'll get that proposal over to you, but so we don't play email tag, let's put a 15-minute placeholder on the calendar for Tuesday to review it together."

If it's not on the calendar, the deal doesn't exist
