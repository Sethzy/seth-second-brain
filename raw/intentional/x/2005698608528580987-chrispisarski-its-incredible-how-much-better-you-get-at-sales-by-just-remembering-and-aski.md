## Verbatim Text

its incredible how much better you get at sales by just remembering and asking the same questions over and over again:

- why do you need to fix this now instead of waiting until next quarter?

- is there a specific date or event driving the timeline for this decision?

- if we fast forward 6 months and nothing has changed, what does that impact look like for you personally?

- when was the last time you bought a tool like this and what steps did you have to go through?

- it sounds like you may have already had a discussion about budget, what did that conversation sound like?

- what is the cost of doing nothing for another 3 months?
