## Verbatim Text

Most AEs uncover pain on a first call. That’s not the mistake. 

It's skipping to ask what happens if nothing changes.

Think about how many first calls end with a good conversation, a "this sounds helpful,” and then... nothing. 

The deal dies because urgency was never established. 

There was never commitment to solve. 

𝗧𝗵𝗶𝘀 𝗾𝘂𝗲𝘀𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗰𝗵𝗮𝗻𝗴𝗲𝘀 𝘁𝗵𝗮𝘁: "I don't want to ask you a loaded question — but what happens if we don't get there?"

Uncomfortable to ask. Impossible to skip if you want to close.

Pair it with: "What's the biggest single risk to making this a success?"

Now you're uncovering the implication of that pain. 

And that’s what drives true urgency.
