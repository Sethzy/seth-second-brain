## Verbatim Text

If you are in a sales call with someone who is not the final decision maker, do this:

1) before the call, map out everyone that needs to be involved for this deal to move forward

2) come in with a hypothesis of why they typically need to be involved, ideally find a really specific connection between you and them (for example: “cmo used to work at the same company”)

3) early in the call, say something like: “typically, the people who want to be involved at this stage are X and Y because of this, I saw that might be alex from your marketing team”

4) at the end of the call, ask to share the recap with those people since they were already brought up

by naming people early, you remove the awkwardness of looping them in later
