## Verbatim Text

I haven’t heard any B2B marketers talk about the ‘silent conversions’ that outbound creates.

In fact, a MAJORITY of outbound attribution models are undercounting.

Most models credit the last touch: 

• The email that got the reply
• The call that booked the meeting.

Etc, etc.

But what about when a prospect: 

> sees your cold email
> doesn't respond
> Googles you
> Signs up for your newsletter

THEN inbounds 6 weeks later.

Your CRM credits the inbound form.

The actual influence of your outbound motion is 2 to 3x what your reporting shows.

If you're making investment decisions based on attributed pipeline alone, you're cutting the channel doing the most invisible work.
