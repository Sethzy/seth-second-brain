## Verbatim Text

YC taught us to never take a sales meeting without asking these 7 questions first:

1) What made you decide to take this call
2) Tell me about the problem. How long have you had it
3) How bad is it? Who else does it affect?
4) How do you quantify the cost/impact of this problem
5) Why haven't you solved it already?
6) What is your budget for solving this?
7) How does your organization buy software? Who makes the decision?
