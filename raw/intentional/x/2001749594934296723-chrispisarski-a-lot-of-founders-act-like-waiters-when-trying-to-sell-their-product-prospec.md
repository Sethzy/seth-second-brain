## Verbatim Text

A lot of founders act like waiters when trying to sell their product

Prospect: "how much is it?"
founder "$10k"
prospect: "okay let me think about it!"
*they never come back*

You lost because you gave the answer before you understood the problem.

Next time someone asks for the price too early, before you even have the chance to talk with them and understand their problem, don't give it to them.

Say this instead:

"I don't even know if I can help you yet. can I ask you a few questions to see if you're even a fit?"

Of course you should be transparent about pricing. but if it’s the first thing you talk about with a prospect without understanding their problem, you’re forcing them to evaluate a price instead of the value
