"I just need to post more on LinkedIn to grow."

The accounts that stop growing after a few weeks all believed exactly that.

When you’re at zero, more posts is the WRONG lever.

Instead: 

→ 3 posts a week
→ run for 12 weeks
→ gather a dataset.

A dataset shows you which topics get saved, which formats get shared, and which posts drive profile visits.

Don't increase frequency until you know what you're looking at.
