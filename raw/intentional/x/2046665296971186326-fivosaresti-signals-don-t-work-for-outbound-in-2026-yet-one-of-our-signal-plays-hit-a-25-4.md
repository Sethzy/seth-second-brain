“signals don’t work for outbound in 2026”

Yet, one of our signal plays hit a 25.4% reply rate.

That is 2x to 10x higher than standard cold outbound.

Here are the 5 signal plays we ran and tracked every reply:

1. Customer alumni
• Pull closed-won accounts from HubSpot
• Clay identifies job changes from those accounts
• Score the new companies for ICP fit
• Route to outreach via Instantly and HeyReach

2. Website visitors
• Warmly identifies visitors who don't convert
• Accounts and contacts pushed to Clay
• High-score leads get a Slack alert for manual outreach
• Low-score leads go into automated sequences

3. Founder LinkedIn connections
• 65,000+ combined followers between founders
• Conversational outreach to existing connections
• This play hit a 25.4% reply rate
• That result came from 1+ year of consistent LinkedIn posting

4. LinkedIn engagement
• Jungler and Teamfluence track likes and profile visitors
• Clay enriches every signal
• Outreach allocated by ICP score

5. Social listening
• Monitor LinkedIn for specific keywords
• Clay captures and enriches matching profiles
• ICP match triggers a targeted campaign

Founder LinkedIn Connections was the best-performing play at 25.4% reply rate.

Cold outbound still works, but signal-based outbound compounds over time.
