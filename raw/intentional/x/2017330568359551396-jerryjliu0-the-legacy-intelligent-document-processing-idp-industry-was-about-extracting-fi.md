## Verbatim Text

The legacy “Intelligent Document Processing” (IDP) industry was about extracting fields.

The new Agentic Document Processing is about *getting work done*.

Understanding a document is a necessary but small step as part of an e2e human workflow. They arrive in packets, require human and machine validation, require semantic reasoning and data validation. The human gathers context from the document and does data entry in a downstream system, or cross-references the context to generate downstream reports.

IDP itself is many billions of $$ in market size, but the space of automating work is exponentially bigger.

LlamaAgents Builder is a first step in that direction: It lets you write down a document workflow, typically performed by humans, in English, and will automatically construct agentic reasoning over these docs in minutes.

It’s early but available to everyone on LlamaCloud, and we’d love your feedback! https://t.co/XYZmx5TFz8
