Teams that rely on only on referrals and word of mouth are going to be left behind in 2027. 

Here’s the outbound system we’re using to generate 20 to 30% of our pipeline:

(add this ASAP)

PHASE 1: Capture and centralize

1) Capture signals from first, second, and third-party sources. Clay sits at the center as the orchestration layer, with RB2B for website visitors, Jungler for LinkedIn signals, and Fibbler for LinkedIn ad engagement feeding in.

2) Aggregate every signal into one place. Clay is where most teams aggregate, with Claude Code as an alternative for custom workflow layers.

3) Normalize the format and deduplicate. The same account often fires three signals on the same day from different sources.

PHASE 2: Enrich and qualify

4) Enrich with company and contact data. Apollo, Findymail, and BetterContact cover contact and email. Sumble handles technographic data.

5) Lookup each signal against HubSpot before acting. You do not want to outbound a prospect already in an active sales cycle.

6) Qualify only ICP-fit signals through Clay's AI qualification or a Claude Code skill.

7) Score each qualified signal into Tier 1, 2, 3, or Unqualified.

PHASE 3: Segment and route

8) Segment by company size, industry, and geography.

9) Route to the right rep based on territory or specialty.

10) Sync the signal back to your CRM as a timeline event.

PHASE 4: Activate and track

11) Activate. Tier 1 signals get manual touches through HubSpot. Tier 2 and 3 get automated sequences through Instantly for email and HeyReach for LinkedIn DMs.

12) Track signals into awareness stages and pipeline reports.

13) Enablement: equip reps with sequences, daily digests, and call scripts.
