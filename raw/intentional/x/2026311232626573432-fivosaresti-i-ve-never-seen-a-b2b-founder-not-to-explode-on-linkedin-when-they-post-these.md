I’ve never seen a B2B founder NOT to explode on LinkedIn when they post these content types consistently for 90 days: 

1. Top-of-Funnel Content (70%)

Main goals:
↳ Create awareness
↳ Build domain authority
↳ Help others know the person behind the screen

Best formats:
• Thought leadership
• Educational
• Giveaways
• Storytelling
• Carousels

2. Middle-of-Funnel Content (20%)

Main goals:
↳ Educate on the problem you solve
↳ Create awareness for your business
↳ Differentiate yourself from the market

Best formats:
• Demos
• How-tos
• Webinars
• Playbooks
• Frameworks

3. Bottom-of-Funnel Content (10%)

Main goals:
↳ Describe your solution in detail
↳ Create hype around launches/releases
↳ Display social proof to drive conversions

Best formats:
• Promotions
• Testimonials
• Case Studies
• Product launches
• Product marketing
