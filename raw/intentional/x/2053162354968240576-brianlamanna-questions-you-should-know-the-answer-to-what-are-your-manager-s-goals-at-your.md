## Verbatim Text

Questions you should know the answer to:

-What are your manager’s goals at your org?
-Exactly what do they need to get promoted?
-Outside of revenue, how is your sales leader measured?
-Where does your manager think your team’s greatest area of opportunity is?
-What’s something you can do that would make your manager’s life easier?

You don’t need manager in your title to operate that way.

Orgs run better when there’s tight alignment between each level.

It’s a 2-way street and you should spend time managing up too.

Carve out 5-10 min in your next 1-on-1 this upcoming week.

They’ll respect you for it.
