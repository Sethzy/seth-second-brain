We cold call leads most teams would never touch again.

They ALL go back into the pipeline.

Here are the 4 categories we segment them into for cold calling:

1. Long term prospects
→ Accounts that showed interest but the timing was wrong.

2. Closed lost > 4 months
→ Enough time has passed for priorities to shift.

3. Partial submissions
→ Someone started a form on our site and abandoned it.

4. No-shows
→ They booked a call and didn't show up.

On top of that, we call:
• ICP cold list
• Repeat website visitors tracked by Warmly
• Qualified newsletter subscribers from beehiiv
• Positive email responders from InstantlyAI

Cold calling is our biggest Q2 priority.

The list you already have is warmer than you think.
