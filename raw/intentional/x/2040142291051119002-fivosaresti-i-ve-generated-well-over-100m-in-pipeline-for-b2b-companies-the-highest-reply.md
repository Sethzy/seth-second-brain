I’ve generated well over $100M in pipeline for B2B companies. 

The highest-reply-rate outbound play we’ve ever run isn't cold.

It's the Customer Alumni Play.

1. Pull all closed-won accounts from CRM for the past 12 to 24 months.

That's your Alumni Seed List.

2. Find everyone who worked at those accounts using Clay.

Some have moved to new companies since. That's your Alumni Signal List.

3. Check whether their new company fits your ICP.

Score and tier them: Tier 1 for manual AE-led outreach, Tier 2 for automated multichannel.

Reply rates run 3-5x higher than standard cold outbound.

The relationship already exists.

You just need to find where it moved.
